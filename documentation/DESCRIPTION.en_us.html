Callisto is an open-source serif typeface designed for easy readability over long stretches of small text in print and on-screen. The typeface follows in the footsteps of commonly-used printers' typefaces such as Monotype's Plantin and the various Garamond typefaces, among many others, in its wide apertures, chunky letterforms and pursuit of readability. Callisto is currently a roman-only typeface, in regular, semibold and bold weights.

Contributions to this project are welcome and encouraged. To contribute, contact Ishtar van Looy.
